Convention 4.1.1. Notation for complex numbers.

While most mathematicians prefer the notation \(z=x+iy\) for a complex number \(z\in\Comps\text{,}\) where \(x,y\in\Reals\text{,}\) programmers, engineers, and scientists have adopted the notation \(z=x+yj\text{.}\) In the interest of being consistent with Python, we will use \(z=x+yj\) notation throughout the text.
