Let \(m,n\in\Zp\) and define \(\Mats{m,n}\) to be the set of all \(m\times n\) matrices over \(\Comps\text{,}\) and define operations of addition and scalar multiplication in \(\Mats{m,n}\) as follows:
Matrix addition
Let \(A_1,A_2\in\Mats{m,n}\text{.}\) Then define \(A_1+A_2\) to be the unique element of \(\Mats{m,n}\) satisfying
\begin{equation*} \entry{A_1+A_2}{r,c} = \entry{A_1}{r,c} + \entry{A_2}{r,c} \end{equation*}
for every \({r\in\set{0,\dotsc,m-1}}\) and \({c\in\set{0,\dotsc,n-1}}\text{.}\)
Scalar-matrix multiplication
Let \(A\in\Mats{m,n}\) and let \(z\in\Comps\text{.}\) Then \(zA\) is the unique element of \(\Mats{m,n}\) satisfying
\begin{equation*} \entry{z A}{r,c} = z\entry{A}{r,c} \end{equation*}
for every \({r\in\set{0,\dotsc,m-1}}\) and \({c\in\set{0,\dotsc,n-1}}\text{.}\)
