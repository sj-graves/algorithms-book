The modification to Dijkstra's algorithm to construct a subgraph consisting of all shortest paths from \(s\) to \(t\) is conceptually deep but easy to implement. Suppose \(v\in V\) with \(\dist{\Delta_f}{s}{v}=k\text{,}\) where \(s\) is the source vertex. Rather than recording a single \(s,v\)-path, we records every vertex \(u\in V\) such that \(\dist{\Delta_f}{s}{u}=k-1\) and there is an arc 3  from \(u\) to \(v\text{.}\)
