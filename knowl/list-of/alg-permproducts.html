Algorithm 2.1.11. Left-multiplication of permutations.
Suppose \(\sigma_1,\sigma_2,\dotsc,\sigma_k\) are permutations of the set \(A=\set{a_1,a_2,\dotsc,a_n}\text{,}\) and further suppose the product \(\sigma_k\circ\sigma_{k-1}\circ\cdots\circ\sigma_2\circ\sigma_1\) is desired.
  1. For each \(i\in [n]\text{,}\) do the following:
    1. Let \(x=a_i\text{.}\)
    2. For each \(j\in[k]\text{,}\) do the following:
      1. Update \(x = \sigma_j(x)\text{.}\)
    3. Write \((\sigma_k\circ\sigma_{k-1}\circ\cdots\circ\sigma_2\circ\sigma_1)(a_i) = x\text{.}\)
