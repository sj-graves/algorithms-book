Theorem 4.3.10. Entries of a matrix-matrix product.

Suppose that \(A\in\Mats{m,n}\) and \(B\in\Mats{n,q}\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} \entry{AB}{r,c} = \sum_{k=0}^{n-1}\entry{A}{r,k}\entry{B}{k,c} \end{equation*}
for each \({r\in\set{0,\dotsc,m-1}}\) and \({c\in\set{0,\dotsc,q-1}}\text{.}\)
