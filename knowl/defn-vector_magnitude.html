Definition 4.1.13. Magnitude in \(\CV{n}\).

The norm of a vector \(\vv\in\CV{n}\) is the scalar quantity
\begin{equation*} \norm{\vv} = \sqrt{\sum_{k=0}^{n-1}\abs{\entry{\vv}{k}}^2}\text{.} \end{equation*}
The value \(\norm{\vv}\in\Reals\text{,}\) and is also called the magnitude of \(\vv\).
