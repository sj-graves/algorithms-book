For each \(j=1,2,\ldots,m\text{,}\) let \(uv=e_j\text{.}\) Do the following:
  1. If \(u\in C_k\) and \(v\in C_\ell\) and \(C_k\neq C_\ell\) where \(k\lt \ell\text{,}\) then let \(C_k = C_k\cup C_\ell\) and let \(E_{j} = E_{j-1}\cup\set{e_j}\text{.}\) Otherwise, let \(E_{j} = E_{j-1}\text{.}\)
