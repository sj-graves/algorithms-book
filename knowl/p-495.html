Let \(A_1,A_2\in\Mats{m,n}\text{.}\) Then define \(A_1+A_2\) to be the unique element of \(\Mats{m,n}\) satisfying
\begin{equation*} \entry{A_1+A_2}{r,c} = \entry{A_1}{r,c} + \entry{A_2}{r,c} \end{equation*}
for every \({r\in\set{0,\dotsc,m-1}}\) and \({c\in\set{0,\dotsc,n-1}}\text{.}\)
